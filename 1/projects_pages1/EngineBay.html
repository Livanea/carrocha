Engine Bay
UI/UX Design

Engine Bay developed by Hureka Technologies.


Car's marketplace app where the users are car's dealerships and dealers to buy, sell, trade, auction, and appraise each other's cars.


The design process revolved around making the experience easy, intuitive, and seamless, by relying on standards UI components and users expectations created by previous experiences with similar apps.
