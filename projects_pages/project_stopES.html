Stop! Endangered Species
Illustration | Branding & Packaging design

Completed as a college project for my Graphic & Multimedia Design B.A.


The briefing revolved around creating graphic material to support a cause that we related to and to generate awareness towards it. As such, I chose animal conservation.


For this project, I illustrated seven animals from Africa, America, Asia and Europe.


Designed posters, postcards, and notebooks with the illustration of the animals and infographics about their situations.


Red = critically endangered;

Yellow = endangered.


Date: 2012
