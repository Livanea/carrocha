Hi, I am Carla!

I am an islander from the Azores islands, Portugal. Thanks to having the ocean and the horizon as my constant background growing up or maybe thanks to the stories of the Portuguese Age of Discovery, or maybe thanks to my interest of legends and stories of faraway lands, I developed a passion for travelling and exploring new cultures.

Besides living in Portugal, both mainland and on the islands, I also lived in Barcelona and in the Netherlands. I arrived in the USA 4 years ago, as an au pair with the intention of learning some programming languages to complement my B.A. in Graphic and Multimedia Design.
