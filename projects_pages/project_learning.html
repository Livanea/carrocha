Educatinal - Learn Portuguese
UI & App Design

Project developed for college, as part of my Interface and Web Development A.S.


The briefing for this project consisted of creating an interactive application targeting a specific audience.


Target audience chosen - children between the ages of 6 and 10.

This originated the idea of creating categories where one could read books, play games (find the differences), and learn Portuguese (with audio and written), and test what one learned.
